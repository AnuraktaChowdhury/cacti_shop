About

Hey,I am Siti.Passionate about cacti and plants, driven by my deep commitment to the environment. Each cactus I nurture reflects my belief in resilience and adaptability, reminding me of the beauty that can thrive in challenging conditions. Through caring for plants, I embrace a sustainable lifestyle, fostering a greener planet one leaf at a time. My love for these remarkable organisms fuels my desire to inspire others to appreciate and protect our natural world.

Culture of conservation

My cacti farm is nestled in the peaceful Tarapur Tea Estate, close to my home in Pathantula. This special location allows me to grow and care for a variety of cacti in a natural, sustainable environment. My farm reflects a deep commitment to conservation, where I strive to harmonize with the surrounding nature and minimize environmental impact. By cultivating resilient, low-maintenance plants like cacti, I contribute to a greener planet while promoting the importance of sustainable farming practices. Each cactus I grow is a testament to the power of nature to adapt and flourish, even in the most unexpected places.
